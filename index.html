Investment Criteria

Avon Capital is a Mid-Atlantic search fund dedicated to acquiring an eduring profitable business that is ready for its next phase of growth. At Avon, we are committed to preserving the legacy and community of your business and will carry it forward with laser focus. Our search criteria includes:

  • Annual Revenue of $8M-$50M; EBITDA of $1.5M-$7M
  • Recurring revenue and a track record of profitable growth year-over-year
  • Differentiation and strong competitive advantage in the marketplace
  • Current leadership is seeking a transition plan that preserves their legacy
